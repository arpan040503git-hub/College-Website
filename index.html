Aurora Institute of Technology College is a center of excellence in higher education, dedicated to nurturing talent, innovation, and leadership. We provide a dynamic learning environment that prepares students for successful careers and meaningful lives.

About Our College

Established in 1998, Aurora Institute of Technology College is known for academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and industry-oriented programs. Our experienced faculty members focus on practical learning, research, and skill development to ensure students are job-ready.

We promote innovation, leadership, and social responsibility through academic and co-curricular activities.

Admissions Open 2026–27

We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to meet industry standards. The admission process is simple, transparent, and merit-based. Scholarships and financial aid are available.

  • BCA, BSc, BCom
  • MCA, MBA, MSc
  • Online Application Facility

Eligibility: Minimum 50% in qualifying examination.
